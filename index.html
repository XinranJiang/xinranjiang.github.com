Documentary / Observational • Role: Producer & Camera

After Arrival: Two Years to Stand (2025/2026)

A refugee in the UK confronts public myths with lived reality, tracing a two-year journey from initial survival to gradual rebuilding, showing how structured support enables agency and participation.

*金史密斯实践视点：本片摒弃了戏剧化的宏大视角，受《For Sama》启发，通过日常生活的微观重复（官僚体制、语言障碍），展现个体在漫长时间轴上的重构过程。

Documentary / Immersive • Role: Sound, Light Director & Camera

Patchwork (2025)

An immersive short documentary exploring the frontier of electronic music through the Goldsmiths Laptop Ensemble, capturing how music is reborn from circuits, code, and collaborative community.

*金史密斯实践视点：聚焦数字艺术与人类情感的边界，尝试将音频波形的多轨律动内化于剪辑节奏之中，用光影捕捉代码的呼吸。

Short Drama / Dark Comedy • Role: Sound & Light Director

ATE? (2025)

A short drama with comedic undertones that follows Penny, an individual who is obsessed with her weight. Set entirely during the night in her flat, the film explores psychological pressures and body anxiety.

*金史密斯实践视点：在单一密闭的室内空间中，通过体重计的幽蓝冷光与环境黄光的剧烈对冲，外化现代个体的内心焦虑与精神规训。
